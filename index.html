About

Hi I'm Lucci, a self-taught front end developer from southern California. I'm passionate about coding clean, functional and beautiful solutions in the form of websites. Currently I work as a freelancer in web development/design and as a graphic designer for an educational YouTube channel with over 100,000 subscribers.

I've been messing around with HTML and CSS since the Myspace days; I was obsessed with page customization. At the end of my senior year in high school, I decided to buckle down and learn how to program. I started out with Python on Codecademy and Coursera, then later took different college courses in C, Java, Ruby, Ruby on Rails and Javascript. However, those courses didn't provide enough depth or practical knowledge for me to feel confident in making my own projects; I didn't know where to start!

After a couple of hiatuses from coding (that were spent working minimum-wage jobs and traveling), I decided it was best for me to narrow my learning to front end development. The artistic design aspects of web development have always appealed to me most. Since then, I've been enthusiastically upgrading my skills with freeCodeCamp, Udemy courses and a few trusting website clients.
